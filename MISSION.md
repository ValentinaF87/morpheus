# Mission

Morpheus exists to help people spend their attention where it matters most.

In contexts of information overload, the hardest part is often not finding more options, but understanding which ones deserve deeper attention.

Morpheus is designed to support that process: it helps reduce noise, surface relevant opportunities, and make decision-making less overwhelming without replacing human judgment.

## North Star

Help people manage their attention better when they have too many opportunities to evaluate.

## Core belief

Better decisions do not come from having more information: they come from knowing what deserves attention.

## First use case

The first use case is Job Discovery.

In this context, Morpheus helps evaluate professional opportunities by filtering job alerts, extracting relevant information, generating explainable recommendations, and collecting human feedback.

The goal is not to automate career decisions.

The goal is to make it easier to decide where to invest time, energy, and attention.
