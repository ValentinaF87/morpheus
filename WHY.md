# WHY

Every project starts with a problem.

For Morpheus, the problem wasn't technical.

It was time.

When my daughter was born, I suddenly found myself with two goals that mattered equally: I wanted to deepen my understanding of Agentic AI, and I was looking for my next professional opportunity. The challenge was that both required the same limited resource.

Time.

During my daughter's naps, I could either watch a course or search for jobs. Every hour spent learning meant one less hour spent applying, and every hour spent job hunting slowed down my learning.

At first, I tried to optimize my schedule.

Eventually, I realized I was trying to solve the wrong problem.

Instead of asking *"How can I find more time?"*, I started asking *"How can I make better use of the time I already have?"*

That shift in perspective led to Morpheus.

The first use case happened to be job discovery, but the idea behind Morpheus has never really been about finding jobs.

It is about reducing information overload so that attention can be spent where it creates the most value.

Looking back, I realized that this idea has quietly shaped my career for years.

The most rewarding part of my work has never been building dashboards or structuring Excel files. It has always been asking the right questions to solve real-life problems.

Morpheus is simply the latest expression of that mindset.
